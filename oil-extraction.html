High-Efficiency Extraction & Refining

Our advanced extraction facility transforms premium Soya beans into high-yield, top-tier commodities, carefully preserving their peak nutritional and industrial value.

Utilizing advanced solvent extraction and refining processes, we ensure our products meet the stringent requirements of both the food processing and animal feed industry.

Core Derivatives:

  • Soya DOC (Normal & High Pro): High-protein, nutritionally balanced de-oiled cake for premium animal and poultry feed.
  • Soya Refined Oil: Ultra-pure, neutral-tasting oil filtered for high-smoke point cooking.
  • Soya Acid Oil: High-efficiency industrial byproduct ideal for soap manufacturing and chemicals.
  • Soya Fatty Oil: Rich in essential fatty acids, utilized extensively in cosmetics, paints, and specialized industrial formulations.
  • Soya Lecithin: Natural emulsifiers essential for food processing and pharmaceuticals.
