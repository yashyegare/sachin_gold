About



Since 1969, Hude Group has been serving the nation with the finest Sortex quality agro commodities, along with premium finished products and related services such as transportation, warehousing, and cold storage. Backed by decades of trust and continuous investment in modern technology, we ensure that every grain reaches our customers with purity, nutrition, and natural goodness.

We are proudly present across Maharashtra and Karnataka in regions including Udgir, Latur, Solapur, Bhalki, and Bidar. Our strong geographical presence allows us to offer high-protein crops of superior quality while being strategically located to ensure easy access and round-the-clock service.

At the heart of our operations is a strong belief in empowering our agricultural community. Through the ITC e-Choupal initiative, we've fostered deep bonds with our growers, successfully connecting over 1.5 lakh farmers and customers to promote sustainable and mutually beneficial trade.

Our state-of-the-art processing units are equipped with advanced machinery and automated sorting systems. We also specialize in soybean extractions and refined edible oil plants, operating under the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness.

With an unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, Hude Group continues to nourish households and industries alike.
